nanogallery2 creates beautiful and high quality photo galleries





nanogallery2 is focused on performance and on providing the best experience to user with any device. Galleries are highly customizable: choose between justified, grid or cascading layout and style it to suit your personnal needs.




Instant ready

You don't need to read the full documentation to start: use the builder to quickly and easily generate HTML templates and publish them on your website in a couple of minutes.



Eye-catching effects

Thousands of transitions and effects combinations to capture the attention of your visitors. Use presets or create complex effects very easily.



Content source

nanogallery2 uses the photos from where you already store them: Flickr, Google Photos or your web server. You don't have to change anything on your current photo management. Just point nanogallery2 to your existing content on the web. Naturally, you can also self host your photos on your personal website. Gallery customization is done regardless of your content source.



Simplify your photo publishing workflow

With nanogallery2, you can publish your photos to your website without any break in your standard way to handle your photos.
- Just push your photos from your mobile phone to Google Photos or Flickr, and they are automatically available on your website. No need to push your photos to your website using FTP.
- More sophisticated workflow can also be imagined: import your DSLR photos to Adobe Lightroom and after processing publish them to Google Photos or Flickr.



Why store your photos on Google Photos or Flickr?

Besides simplifying your workflow, there's one unique feature: images are automatically optimized for better performance. Concretely, this means that you don't need to generate the thumbnails of your photos. It's even perfect for responsive design: the best thumbnail size is dynamically and automatically generated, to fit any page layout, on any device. You just have to define thumbnails sizes in the nanogallery2's options.



What's new since nanoGALLERY?

nanogallery2 is following the popular nanoGALLERY script. The display algorithm has been completly rewritten for a better user experience. New features include: filter by keywords, shopping cart, social sharing, advanced hover effects, styling, tools on thumbnails, more button, thumbnail display transitions, navigation in albums without breadcrumb, info page with google maps location...
